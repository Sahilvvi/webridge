What is Search Engine Marketing (SEM)?

Search Engine Marketing (SEM) is a powerful digital marketing strategy that uses paid advertising on search engines like Google to increase visibility, drive instant traffic, and generate high-quality leads.

Unlike organic methods, SEM delivers immediate results by placing your business at the top of search engine results pages (SERPs). It includes tactics such as pay-per-click (PPC) advertising, keyword targeting, ad optimization, and performance tracking.

SEO vs SEM:
SEO focuses on long-term organic growth, while SEM provides instant visibility and faster results through paid campaigns.
